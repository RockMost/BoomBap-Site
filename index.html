Welcome

Welcome to Back To The Boom Bap — a high-energy celebration of the Golden Era of Hip Hop, R&B, and Dancehall! a high-energy dance party celebrating the Golden Era of Hip Hop, R&B, and Dancehall — the years 1988 to 1995. Featuring a curated lineup of skilled DJs, the event brings nostalgic vibes, authentic music, and a welcoming, energetic atmosphere to the dancefloor.

For a decade, Back to the Boom Bap (formerly Boom Bap Saturday), a legendary old-school and classic hip-hop event in Atlanta, brought together a diverse crowd of music lovers, creating an electric atmosphere of nostalgia and pure hip-hop energy. Known for its infectious vibe, the event celebrated the golden era of hip-hop with phenomenal DJs spinning classic tracks, live performances, and an undeniable sense of community.

It became a staple in the city's nightlife, attracting everyone from longtime hip-hop heads to newcomers eager to experience the culture. Year after year, people came out to vibe, dance, and connect, making it one of Atlanta's most beloved and enduring hip-hop gatherings.
